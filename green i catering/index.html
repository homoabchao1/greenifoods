About us


Honest bread since 2012

The Green i Society began in Cayuga, Ontario while the founder was living as grid free as he could. The pure ways of making what he and his friends ate and enjoyed have reached you here in the form of a menu. It is our sincere hope that you might share our delight at these humbly curated meals. Serving your good health, sensibilities and tastes have been our goals in creating what you have here before you. Bon Apetit!

The carbonated beverage menu you see here is a result of relentless experimentation and research. Oppinions and personalities of every imaginable kind have contributed to the selection of delightful mixtures weve settled on. whether classic or original and inventive, the power to create a favourite is at your fingertips.
